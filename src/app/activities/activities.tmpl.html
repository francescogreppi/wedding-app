What to eat and drink?

Are you doing diet? Forget it, for a couple of days at least. Lake Como area has a lot to offer in terms of food and drink, just sit down and let yourself go. From lake fish to local meat, cheese and wine, there's plenty of culinaires experiences you can do.
The north of the lake open it's way to Valtellina, a spectacular area known for its wines and food tradition.
