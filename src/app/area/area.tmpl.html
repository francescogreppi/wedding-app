How to reach Lake Como?

Suggestions: the best way to move around is by car (see useful links below).

Unfortunately there are not direct public transport connections from Milan Malpensa airport to Tremezzina area. However if you are not in a rush, you can do the following once you landed at Malpensa Airport:

- take a train from Malpensa Airport to Milan Central Station
- take a train from Milan Central Station to Varenna
- take a ferry from Varenna either to Menaggio, or Lenno, or Tremezzo, according to your stay.

In the map below you'll find indications from Milan Malpensa Airport by car.
