How to reach Lake Como?

1. Which is the closest airport?

Book a flight: the closest airport destination is Milan Malpensa.
See the useful links below to book a flight.

2. How to reach Lake Como?

Suggestions: the best way to move around is by car (see useful links below for car rental).

Unfortunately there are not direct public transport connections from Milan Malpensa airport to Tremezzina area.
However if you are not in a rush, you can do the following once you landed at Malpensa Airport:

- take a train from Malpensa Airport to Milan Central Station
- take a train from Milan Central Station to Varenna
- take a ferry from Varenna either to Menaggio, or Lenno, or Tremezzo, according to your stay.
