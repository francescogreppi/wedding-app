Where should I stay?

Well, the good news is that everything is in the range of 10 kilometers. The bad news is public transport is not as efficient as in a city. However here's some indications.

Menaggio is the village where the party will take place, it could be a good choice for if you don't want to move too much after having a few drinks.

Lenno, Tremezzo, Griante, Ossuccio and Mezzegra are all villages adjacent to Menaggio, recently unified in a single municipality known as Tremezzina. If you are coming by car, or planning to rent a bike/scooter to move around then they can be a good choice.

Tremezzo is probably the most charming between the villages, and it's where the civil ceremony will take place.
