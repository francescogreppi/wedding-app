Discover Lake Como

Lake Como is known for its beautiful landscape, romantic villas and the spectacular backdrop of the Grigna mountains.

Menaggio, Tremezzo and Lenno sit on the sunny western shore and have beautiful lakeside promenades. Have a walk, take a sit on a café, enjoy a good glass of wine and admire the fantastic views across the water.

On the southern shore you'll find Bellagio, probably the most famous location in lake Como. Thanks to its particular position you can enjoy breath taking views across the lake from both sides of the town.

Lake Como is surrounded by high mountains and cliffs, this make it different from the other Italian lakes and unique for the range of experiences it can offer to its visitors. A narrow road runs up its western coast towards Switzerland and sits up high above the water, with the towns dropping down beneath it. Famous for its beautifully kept public gardens and historic villas, it's easy to see why this location is considered one of the most charming in Europe.
